Hello from Lady B

About Bushin

Hi Everyone, please call me Lady B. I was born in Liberia and raised both in Liberia and America. I love meeting new people with the hope that I can add something positive to their lives and they can add to mine as well. Of all my hobbies, I love to read, travel and exploring the outdoors. I have a driven passion for braiding hair and I also love helping people look and feel good about their hair. Your hair plays a big role in how you feel about your day and I'm so glad you chose me to be your braid stylist.

Policy

A deposit is required for all bookings. Your deposit will be deducted from your total cost. Deposits are absolutely NON-REFUNDABLE. If you cancel your appointment 72 hours before the time of your service, you will be issued a credit and your deposit can be used towards another appointment booked within 30 days of your cancellation. Deposits are NOT CREDITED and NON-TRANSFERABLE for appointment booked less than 72 hours to the time of service or appointment canceled less than 72 hours to the time of your service. If your appointment is canceled due to my faults, you may choose to reschedule or be fully refunded.

COVID-19 Ready

A face mask is recommended at your appointment. Please do not bring children or extra guest with you to your appointment. If you have a cough, fever, loss of taste or have recently came in contact with anyone who tested positive for COVID-19, please call to reschedule your appointment within two weeks of your cancellation. We are dedicated to giving you the best experience at our business with cleanliness and safety. We are following guidelines to protect against the spread of COVID-19.
